ABOUT ME


Simply put, I am a lover of puzzles and outdoors. Most of my major life choices are centered around these things -- guiding me in my career aspirations, where I live, how I travel, and which hobbies I participate in. Below is an outline of my journey, leading to where I am today!



1985-2003 >> I grew up in Saint Charles, IL and enjoyed hobbies like cross-country skiing and horse-back riding. My favorite subject in High School was math (surprise, surprise!)


2003-2010 >> I went to Iowa State University and studied Mathematics. After undergrad, I stayed at Iowa State for my Masters in Applied Mathematics. I was also fortunate to work for the Iowa State Outdoor Recreated Program during this time and led outdoors trips to various destinations (including rock-climbing in Death Valley, Canyoneering in Zion, and Rafting the Green River).


2010-2013 >> After grad school, I moved to Denver, CO. I had known for a long time that I wanted to live near the mountains and I was so excited to finally take this step. I also started a career teaching High School and Community College mathematics. There are many things that I love and miss about teaching, however I decided I wanted to pursue a career which presents more opportunity for new, ever-changing challenges.


2013-2017 >> I began a new career as a data analyst and trully enjoyed learning a variety of new skills. I found that I most enjoyed any project that allowed me to write any sort of code, which consisted mostly of SQL and VBA for the analyst role. At the end of 2017, I began to consider another career shift to software development so that I could work in a world centered around coding. Over these years, I also began to do more solo adventuresm exploring longer and deeper in the mountains.


2018-current >> Over the course of 2018, I began looking into different areas of software development that I might be most interested in. I started self-teaching python, and really enjoyed the learning and problem-solving. By the end of the year, I decided to speed up my entry to a software development career and signed up for the Tech Academy Bootcamp, which I am currently enrolled in.
